What is Forecastr?

Forecastr is an android app designed to make it easy to view your favourite spots. Targeted at sports where the weather is key to a good session forecastr makes it quick to see which spot is best all on one screen. Forecastr includes wind, weather, swell and tide times all in one place.

How to Install

Before you download the app please make sure you have your device set to allow apps from unknown sources as this app is not in the Google Play store. There is a useful guide to do this HERE.

Then on your Android device click the 'install now' button below to download the APK and start the install process. Alternatively you can download the APK on your PC and email it to your device.

About Me

My name is James Grant, landboarder, kitesurfer, rock climber, cycler and developer from the West Country. I build android apps, ios apps and websites and this is my first android app publicly out in the wild. When I'm not working you will usually find me out on my bike or at one of Bristols climbing centers.

Most of the sports I do rely on knowing what the weather is up to and, as I do lots of sports in lots of places, I wanted to be able to check out the weather for all the spots quickly and easily. This is the primary reason why Forecastr was created and I use it daily to work out if I'm going cycling, kiting or climbing and I hope it can help you out to.
